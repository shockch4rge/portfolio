## Introduction

### THE DISCORD BOT

A month after making the Android app, I decided to develop the Discord bot, mainly for 2 reasons:

1. I was curious on how I could bring this concept from one platform to another.
2. Most Discord music bots were on the verge of shutting down.

The second reason was a big one. YouTube had begun cracking down on Discord bots that played music via their platform by streaming MP3
downloads. Out of the blue, this was a huge no-no for them, so many bots began receiving cease-and-desist letters from Big Video. My
friends and I knew that we were running out of alternatives, so I decided to tackle the creation of one myself.

It was quite rough.
